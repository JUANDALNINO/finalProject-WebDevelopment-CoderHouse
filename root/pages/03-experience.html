land

Today, Paleochora is an ideal destination for alternative activities like walking and trekking all through the year. From beaches, to gorges, remote islands and coastal trails and villages, the southern part of Chania is an unspoiled, remote ecosystem that evokes emotions and offers you memories of a lifetime!

Activities

It is the time when nature awakens and overwhelms us with vibrant smells and colors. This season is perfectly balanced between warm days and chilly nights, making it ideal for long walks and trekking in nature. The world-famous Samaria Gorge, the longest gorge in Europe, opens for visitors and numerous other trails await you.
